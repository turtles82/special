Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Edpuzzle Answers Script ("we," "us," "our," or "Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our bookmarklet and web-based services.

Important: This tool is intended for educational purposes only. Users are responsible for ensuring they use this tool in compliance with their school's academic integrity policies and local laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Edpuzzle Credentials

  • Temporary Storage: When you use our service, we may cache your Edpuzzle authentication tokens to provide you with faster access to answers and assignment data.
  • Cache Duration: These tokens are automatically refreshed and cleared every 10 minutes. We do not permanently store your passwords or login credentials.
  • No Login Password Storage: We never receive, store, or log your Edpuzzle login password.

2.2 Assignment Data

  • We process assignment information (questions, answers, videos) temporarily to extract and display answers to you.
  • This data is not permanently stored on our servers unless explicitly cached for technical purposes.

2.3 Usage Information

  • We may log API requests to monitor service health and detect abuse.
  • Logs may include timestamps, request types, and status codes but do not include personal identifying information.

2.4 Web Version Information

If you use our web-based version:

  • Email address (for account creation and token generation)
  • Session tokens (temporary, automatically expire after 24 hours)
  • Device information may be stored for the "Remember this device" feature

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your requests to the Edpuzzle API
  • To monitor and prevent abuse or unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations or defend against legal claims

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Edpuzzle API: We fetch assignment data from Edpuzzle's official API. Refer to Edpuzzle's Privacy Policy for how they handle your data.
  • Google Gemini AI: For open-ended question responses, we may send question text to Google's Gemini API. Refer to Google's AI Privacy Policy for details.
  • Browser Local Storage: We store session data locally on your device; this data is not sent to our servers unless you explicitly use the web version.

5. Data Retention

  • Temporary Cache: Authentication tokens are kept for a maximum of 10 minutes.
  • Session Tokens (Web Version): Expire and are deleted after 24 hours of inactivity.
  • Logs: Server logs are kept for up to 30 days for debugging and abuse detection purposes.
  • Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your assignment answers, credentials, or personal data.

6. Data Security

  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between you and our servers.
  • Session tokens are stored securely and automatically expire.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., Edpuzzle API calls).
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Use the Service at your own risk.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You have the right to know what data we collect about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your session data at any time.
  • Data Portability: You can export your session information if needed.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the Service at any time, and cached data will be automatically cleared.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us via GitHub issues or Discord (see main repository for contact information).
